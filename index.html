Trendy Interior Design Styles

Explore the most influential interior design styles of today, each with its unique characteristics and aesthetic appeal. From minimalist to maximalist, discover the perfect style for your space.

Japandi

A fusion of Japanese and Scandinavian design principles, Japandi combines minimalism with natural elements to create serene, functional spaces.

  • Natural materials (wood, bamboo)
  • Neutral color palette
  • Clean lines and simple forms
  • Handcrafted elements

Featured Designer: Norm Architects - Copenhagen meets Tokyo collection

Biophilic Design

Bringing the outdoors in, biophilic design emphasizes the connection between nature and built environments to enhance wellbeing.

  • Abundant natural light
  • Living walls and indoor plants
  • Natural textures and materials
  • Organic shapes and forms

Featured Project: Amazon Spheres by NBBJ

Modern Mediterranean

A contemporary take on traditional Mediterranean style, featuring warm textures, earthy tones, and clean lines.

  • Arched doorways and windows
  • Textured walls
  • Terracotta and earth tones
  • Modern furniture with rustic touches

Featured Designer: Kelly Wearstler - Santa Monica Project

New Art Deco

A modern interpretation of the glamorous Art Deco era, combining bold geometrics with contemporary comfort.

  • Bold geometric patterns
  • Luxurious materials (velvet, brass)
  • Jewel tones
  • Statement lighting fixtures

Featured Designer: India Mahdavi - Sketch London
